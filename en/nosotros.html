At Yo Soy, we help lower the rates of breast and cervical cancer in vulnerable populations in Colombia and soon Latam, through prevention, telemedicine, and access to female health education.

Mantra

"Breaking taboos and healing minds through the democratization of female health using technology, AI, and telemedicine so that cancer prevention is accessible to all."

Vision

"By 2030, to be the leading platform in Latin America that reduces the gap in female and mental health of vulnerable women, connecting them with resources, professionals, and job opportunities."

Pioneers in Social Inclusion

At Yo Soy, we believe in the power of inclusion and technology to transform lives. Our mission is to empower women in vulnerable situations, providing them with access to learning and personal development tools through the use of artificial intelligence and data analysis.

We help lower breast and cervical cancer rates in vulnerable populations in Colombia and soon Latam, through prevention, telemedicine, and access to female health education.
